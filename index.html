Welcome to your Charming Maltese Retreat

Welcome to our holiday apartment in Mellieħa, Malta, perfectly located along the scenic road that leads down to the island's largest sandy beach — Mellieħa Bay (Għadira Bay). From the private balcony, you can take in breathtaking views of the historic Mellieħa parish church on the hill and enjoy the relaxed Mediterranean atmosphere.


This apartment is perfect for couples, families, or groups looking for a comfortable and convenient holiday rental in Mellieħa. With its prime location near Għadira Bay, you'll be just minutes away from sandy beaches, local restaurants, shops, and easy bus connections to Valletta, Mdina, and Gozo.

Discover Beaches, Nature and Cultural Sites Around You!

  • Golden Bay (Sandy) – One of Malta's top sandy beaches with golden-tinted sand, clear waters, amenities, and amazing sunsets.
  • Għajn Tuffieħa Bay (Sandy, Quieter) – Serene and less crowded, with stunning views. Note: 200 steps to access.
  • Riviera Beach (Sandy) – Smaller and quieter, ideal for a peaceful escape.
  • Mellieħa Bay / Għadira Bay (Sandy, Family-Friendly) – Malta’s largest sandybeach, ideal for families and shallow swims.
  • Armier & Little Armier Bays (Rocky/Sandy) – Peaceful, crystal-clear waters perfect for swimming and relaxing.
  • Marfa Bay & Secluded Rocky Spots – Quiet coves like Slugs Bay or Dahlet ix-Xmajjar, perfect for snorkeling or solitude.
  • Blue Lagoon (Comino) – 15 min ferry ride; famous turquoise waters, ideal for swimming or boat trips. Need to book for free access bluelagooncomino.mt
  • Gozo Beaches – A short ferry trip to explore Gozo’s beautiful bays and beaches(15 minutes by bus to catch the ferry)

  • Il-Majjistral Nature &; History Park – Coastal trails, native flora, and breathtaking cliff views.
  • Foresta 2000 Nature Reserve – Perfect for a peaceful walk or birdwatching.
  • Selmun Family Park – Great for walks or a picnic.
  • Ta’ Qali Picnic Area – Ideal for family outings and a relaxing afternoon.
  • Għadira Nature Reserve (Sep–May) – A tranquil spot with migratory birds and walking paths.
  • Saint Agatha’s Tower (The Red Tower) – 17th-century tower with panoramic views of Mellieħa, Comino, and Gozo.
  • Popeye Village – Original set from the 1980 'Popeye' movie, now a fun park with colorful houses and boat rides.
  • Il-Madonna tal-Għar (Our Lady of the Grotto Shrine) – A serene spiritual site in Mellieħa.
  • WWII Shelters in Mellieħa - Explore tunnels and learn about Malta’s wartime history.
